The datasets contain 15mm-by-15mm field-of-view cropped images, centered on distinct lesions, extracted from 3D total body photographs. Datasets were curated by the International Skin Imaging Collaboration (ISIC) from images contributed by: Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (USA), Hospital Clínic de Barcelona (Spain), The University of Queensland (Australia), Medical University of Vienna (Austria), University of Athens (Greece), Melanoma Institute Australia (Australia), University Hospital of Basel (Switzerland), Alfred Hospital (Australia), and FNQH Cairns (Australia).

A dataset descriptor covering the SLICE-3D dataset was published in Scientific Data and is openly available at https://www.nature.com/articles/s41597-024-03743-w.
